About the Author

Hello! My name is Kyle, and this is my page to tell you a little about myself. If you'd like to see my resume feel free to download that here. Lately I've been having a blast getting back into coding by learning the various languages of the web in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I'm hoping to find a career utiliziing these skills and growing even more as a developer.

I live in Raleigh, North Carolina with my wife Kaye and my dog Ahri (known by most as red dog). On an average week night you can probably find me sitting on the couch with the dog working through a puzzle from Cracking the Cryptic and answering questions about my wife's newfound obsession: Pokemon Go. I also love to cook (Indian and Korean especially) and have gotten into homebrewing a variety of beers, kombuchas, and meads.

When I'm not in the house you may find me playing disc golf, exploring new restaurants, or dragging my friends to breweries to play board games and grab a beer. As an avid traveler, I'm always keen to take a trip, whether that's a weekend stay in Asheville, or grabbing my passport and jetting off to Tokyo to justify all of the hours in college I spent learning Japanese (まだ練習が必要あるんですが。。。).
